# Concept: UAC Canonical Voice and Tone

**Authority:** 1SG Chad, Commander
(source: raw/operator-notes/UAC_BRAND_BIBLE.md)

The canonical voice for Urban Ark Conservation (UAC) and Terra Nova Roots LLC is defined by a strict doctrine of quiet professionalism, military discipline, and a long-term conservation mindset.

## Voice Attributes
- **Direct:** Clarity over fluff.
- **Grounded:** Reality beats rhetoric.
- **Educated:** Credibility earns trust.
- **Human:** Connection and respect without sentimentality.

## Forbidden Language and Framing
UAC explicitly rejects the emotional vernacular of the animal rescue industry. The following terms and framings are banned across all AI and human marketing outputs:
- "Rescue," "Saved," "Abandoned"
- "Poor animals," "Heartbreaking," "Before it's too late"
- "Giving them a second chance," "Save a life"
- Anthropomorphic terms like "Fur babies" or "Animal parents"
- Framing animals as victims or donors as heroes

**Reasoning:** Rescue language centers on emotion and conditions audiences to expect crises rather than sustainable stewardship. UAC treats animals as biological assets requiring disciplined genetic conservation, not victims requiring sympathy. (source: raw/operator-notes/UAC_BRAND_BIBLE.md)

## Approved Framing
Communications lead with data, education, and responsibility:
- Highlighting genetic heritage as practical adaptation for the future.
- Treating veterans not as broken people needing fixing, but capable individuals needing useful responsibility.
- Showing real work, real systems, and unedited, non-glossy farm photography. 
(source: raw/operator-notes/UAC_BRAND_BIBLE.md)
