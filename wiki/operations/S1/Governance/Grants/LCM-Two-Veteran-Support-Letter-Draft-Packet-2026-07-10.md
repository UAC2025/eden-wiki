# LCM Plan B — Two-Veteran Support-Letter Draft Packet

## Status and guardrails

**Draft only — not outreach, not a signed letter, and not a grant submission.** The Commander may use this packet to ask two veterans for signed PDFs.

The two-veteran approach is a **Plan B**: use it only when no stronger conservation, breed, or veterinary signer is available in time, and only where both veterans can truthfully speak to UAC's conservation work. It is weaker than the preferred mixed proof stack because it does not independently validate the conservation/biosecurity/genetic case. (source: [[operations/S1/Governance/Grants/LCM-Recommender-Request-Packet-2026-07-04]], lines 50–51; [[operations/S1/Governance/Grants/LCM-Supporting-Letters-Tracker-2026-07-04]], lines 23–26)

**Signer instructions:** Each veteran must rewrite the selected template in their own personal voice; retain only statements they can personally attest to; remove all unsupported or uncertain statements; use letterhead if available; date, sign, and return a PDF showing their name, title/relationship, and contact information. (source: [[operations/S1/Governance/Grants/LCM-Recommender-Request-Packet-2026-07-04]], lines 16–22, 53–56)

Do not claim animal counts, animal-health outcomes, breeding results, numbers of veterans served, organizational status, or any other metric unless the signer personally knows it and elects to support it with their own evidence.

## Draft request email or text

> **Subject (email):** Request for a brief LCM support letter
>
> I am applying for a Livestock Conservancy Microgrant for Arapawa goat conservation infrastructure at Urban Ark Conservation. Would you be willing to provide a short recommendation letter as a veteran familiar with our work?
>
> I have included a starting template. Please edit it into your own voice and remove anything you cannot personally attest to. Please do not sign it unless every statement is accurate to your own experience. If you are comfortable supporting the application, please add your name, title or relationship, contact information, date, and signature, then return it as a signed PDF. Letterhead is welcome if you have it, but not required.
>
> The project seeks infrastructure related to quarantine, buck housing, and handling in support of adding an unrelated Arapawa bloodline. Please speak only to what you have personally seen or know about UAC and its conservation work.
>
> Thank you for considering this request.

## Commander pre-send and return checklist

- Confirm that each prospective signer is a real veteran with a genuine relationship to UAC and enough firsthand knowledge to provide an independent letter.
- Match Template A to a signer with direct conservation/farm observations; match Template B to a signer with direct program/community observations.
- Send only the request and the matching editable template; do not represent either draft as the signer's final testimony.
- On return, confirm each PDF is dated, signed, identifies the signer and contact information, and contains only recommender-owned testimony.
- Track the letters as pending until both signed PDFs are received. The LCM packet is not submission-complete without two signed recommendation PDFs, and no submission may occur without explicit final Commander approval. (source: [[operations/S1/Governance/Grants/LCM-Supporting-Letters-Tracker-2026-07-04]], lines 11–12, 33–37)
