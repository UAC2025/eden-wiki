## Eligibility — applicant
- Resident of PA / nonprofit educational institution operating in PA.
- **Nonprofit Educational Institution:** nonprofit org in PA that (1) demonstrates capacity for ag research/education, (2) has experience in sustainable-ag research/education, (3) qualifies as 501(c)(3).

## Eligibility — project geography (CRITICAL)
- All projects must lie within EPA **Most Effective Basins (MEBs)** regions and address reduction of nitrogen, phosphorus, and sediment pollution.
- **Bradford County (Towanda) = PARTIAL eligibility** — only the highlighted MEB area of the county qualifies.
- Must verify the specific project site (e.g., Arapawa breeding center parcel) on the PA AG eligibility map: https://gis.agriculture.pa.gov/SustainableAgricultureGrantEligibility/
- ⚠️ If the farm parcel is outside the MEB highlight, this grant is NOT pursuable. VERIFY before drafting.

## Eligible project purposes (must do ≥1)
1. Reduce operating expenses through ecologically beneficial practices.
2. Improve soil quality and fertility.
3. Improve water quality.
4. Reduce/eliminate need for off-farm inputs (synthetic fertilizer/pesticide).

→ The Arapawa conservation breeding center (rotational grazing, soil building, manure mgmt) maps cleanly to #2/#3/#4. Strong thematic fit IF geography clears.
