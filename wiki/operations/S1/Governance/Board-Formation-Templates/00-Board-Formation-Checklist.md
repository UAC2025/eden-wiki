# UAC Board Formation Checklist

> **DRAFT ONLY — NOT ADOPTED.** This template is for Commander/accountant/legal review. It does not appoint, authorize, adopt, sign, submit, or create legal effect until reviewed and executed by authorized humans.

## Decisions needed

- [ ] Confirm whether UAC will have members or be board-governed without members.
- [ ] Choose initial directors.
- [ ] Choose officers: President, Secretary, Treasurer, other.
- [ ] Review whether existing bylaws should be adopted, revised, or replaced.
- [ ] Review whether Board Charter should be adopted, revised, or replaced.
- [ ] Confirm current registered/principal office.
- [ ] Confirm PA charitable registration / BCO status.
- [ ] Confirm who may prepare filings and who may sign filings.
- [ ] Confirm bank/account authority.

## Formation meeting agenda draft

1. Call meeting to order.
2. Confirm quorum/participants.
3. Review articles, IRS determination, bylaws draft, board charter draft.
4. Elect/appoint officers.
5. Adopt or defer bylaws.
6. Adopt or defer conflict-of-interest policy.
7. Adopt or defer document retention policy.
8. Adopt or defer banking authority.
9. Adopt or defer filing/compliance authority.
10. Assign compliance follow-ups.
11. Adjourn.
