# Document Retention Policy Template

> **DRAFT ONLY — NOT ADOPTED.** This template is for Commander/accountant/legal review. It does not appoint, authorize, adopt, sign, submit, or create legal effect until reviewed and executed by authorized humans.

## Draft retention schedule

| Record type | Minimum retention | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Articles/bylaws/determination letters | Permanent | Corporate record. |
| Board minutes/resolutions | Permanent | Corporate record. |
| IRS filings/acceptance notices | Permanent or 7+ years | Confirm with accountant. |
| Donation records/acknowledgments | 7 years | Confirm threshold rules. |
| Grant agreements/reports | 7 years after closeout | Confirm funder terms. |
| Insurance policies | 7 years after expiration | Keep active declarations accessible. |
| Personnel/volunteer waivers | 7 years or longer | Legal review. |
