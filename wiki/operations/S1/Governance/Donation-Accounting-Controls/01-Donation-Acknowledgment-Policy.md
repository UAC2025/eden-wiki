# UAC Donation Acknowledgment Policy

> **DRAFT CONTROL DOCUMENT — NOT LEGAL/TAX ADVICE.** S-1 may use this internally as a control framework. External use, donor sends, grant submissions, filings, or official adoption require Commander approval and appropriate reviewer input.

## Purpose
Urban Ark Conservation Inc. acknowledges donations with accurate source-backed records while preserving donor trust, nonprofit compliance, and UAC/TNR entity separation.

## Scope
Applies to monetary gifts, in-kind gifts, grant funds, sponsorships recorded as donations, and donor-restricted gifts to UAC. Does not apply to Terra Nova Roots LLC sales, farm product revenue, or commercial transactions.

## Receipt rule
S-1 may prepare receipts from verified records. S-1 may send receipts only when:

1. donor identity is known or intentionally anonymous,
2. donation amount/date/source are verified,
3. entity is UAC, not TNR,
4. any goods/services exchange is recorded,
5. Commander has approved the send workflow or specific receipt,
6. the sent receipt is archived.

## No-estimate rule
Financial totals and receipt amounts must come from source records: bank record, payment processor export, check image/log, donor letter, grant agreement, or other retained evidence. No totals from memory.

## Goods/services statement
Each receipt must state whether UAC provided goods or services in exchange. If unknown, the receipt remains draft until resolved.

## Restricted gifts
If a donor restricts use, record the restriction and track separately until spent/reported.
