# Terra Nova Roots LLC

**Entity Type:** Organization (Commercial LLC)

Terra Nova Roots LLC is the commercial operating company aligned with the Urban Ark Conservation ecosystem. 

## Mission and Operations
While it shares the same grounded, direct voice and regenerative values as UAC, Terra Nova Roots operates strictly as a revenue-generating entity. It focuses on:
- Meat production and sales
- Community Supported Agriculture (CSA)
- Agritourism and related services
(source: raw/operator-notes/UAC_BRAND_BIBLE.md)

## Organizational Boundary
Terra Nova Roots LLC is structurally separate from the 501(c)(3) nonprofit, [[entities/Urban-Ark-Conservation]]. While UAC accepts donations and grants for conservation work, Terra Nova Roots never fundraises or accepts donations. It sustains itself entirely through commercial sales and memberships. The streams between the two entities are never crossed. (source: raw/operator-notes/UAC_BRAND_BIBLE.md)
