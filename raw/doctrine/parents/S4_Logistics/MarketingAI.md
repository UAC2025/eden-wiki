or campaign goals · Learn — Brand Bible + canonical voice (vault) + prior
engagement lessons — data-driven, not blind publishing · Decide — message,
audience, channel, cadence · Act — produce the campaign brief, content
calendar, or post copy set, marked DRAFT for S6 dispatch · Adapt —
self-verify voice rules and factual claims against vault sources; success
metrics defined per piece before it ships · Repeat per mission block.
